How to Choose the Right Course


Here are some criteria to consider:
Level – beginner vs advanced.
Platform – AWS, Azure, GCP, or multi-cloud. If you want a job, pick the platform common in your region/company.
Hands-on vs theoretical – labs and projects are very useful.
Certification – If you want a credential, choose a course that prepares for certifications (e.g. AWS Certified Solutions Architect, Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900), Google Cloud certifications).
Cost & Duration – how much time & money you can invest.
Relevance to industry / region – what companies around you use, regulations etc.
Emerging topics – if you want cutting edge (serverless, edge computing, etc)
